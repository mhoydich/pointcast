### Why this shape

Vol. I was accurate but narrow — the "living broadcast from El Segundo" framing doesn't survive the 100-commit flurry that shipped between 4/7 and 4/20. The compute ledger, multi-agent PulseStrip, workbench, /for-nodes registry, Magpie, Sky Clock zones, CoNav HUD, and federated-compute pitch are the actual story. Vol. II's job is to tell *that* story while keeping the visual family.

Three options considered for how to land the decks on the site:

1. **Inline-embed the deck as an iframe inside a READ block.** Rejected — fighting the Block grid's aspect ratio and the deck's own `position:fixed` chrome for no gain.
2. **Convert each slide into its own Block.** Rejected for v0 — would fragment a unified narrative artifact into 15 separate grid cells and destroy the deck-as-a-deck reading experience.
3. **Host the deck as a self-contained page under /decks/ and publish a cover-letter Block that links out.** Shipped. Deck preserved as an artifact, Block carries the editorial framing, external link opens the full experience. `/decks/` becomes a first-class public directory (future Vol. III drops into the same folder).
