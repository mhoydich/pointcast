# PointCast SEO Audit — 2026-04-21

**Audit type:** Full site · **Domain:** pointcast.xyz · **Goal:** organic traffic growth.

---

## Executive summary

PointCast has one of the strongest **agent-era SEO foundations** I've seen on a personal site: `llms.txt` + `llms-full.txt`, Content-Signals opt-in, a consolidated `/agents.json` manifest, stripped-HTML mode keyed on User-Agent, per-channel JSON+RSS feeds, and a clean JSON-LD graph (WebSite + Organization + Person + CollectionPage + BreadcrumbList + ItemList + FAQPage + DefinedTermSet). The technical scaffolding is ~90% of the way to ideal. **The bottleneck is not infrastructure — it's content surface area and keyword targeting.** Most blocks are too short to rank, channel pages have one-sentence descriptions, and the two highest-opportunity keyword clusters (El Segundo local + "agent-native web" / "llms.txt example") have no dedicated landing pages. Top 3 priorities: (1) turn channel pages into real landing pages, (2) ship a `/el-segundo` local-SEO pillar, (3) upgrade READ-block schema from `CreativeWork` to `Article`/`BlogPosting` and land a thought-leadership pillar at `/agent-native`.

**Overall assessment:** strong foundation, content surface area needs work.
