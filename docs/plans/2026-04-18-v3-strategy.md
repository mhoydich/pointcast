## Four threads

### 1 · DAO — governance surface

Not another governance-theatre token. A DAO for PointCast should answer one question: *what decisions does the community actually get to make?*

Candidate decisions:

- **Card of the Day roster** — which Nouns enter the 21-card Battler rotation. Today the list is hand-curated in `src/lib/battler/card-of-the-day.ts`. DAO vote could add/remove entries.
- **Faucet selection** — which tokenId PointCast mints free that day. Today it rotates mechanically; DAO could pick the week's theme.
- **Prize Cast parameters** — draw cadence (weekly vs biweekly), minimum deposit, platform fee %, or "pause the machine for a week and roll the prize over."
- **Channel curation** — add a 10th channel? (CH.CST for Cast-native content? CH.MSH for mesh?) Schema-level votes.
- **Treasury allocations** — when the site earns yield (see thread 2), where does the overflow go? Community devs? Nouns DAO? Seed the next round?

**Primitive candidates:**

- **Lite, Snapshot-style off-chain voting** — Tezos-signed messages, no gas, indexed server-side. Fast.
- **On-chain FA2 governance token** — one vote per token (or quadratic by held-amount). Heavyweight.
- **Merge with DRUM Token** — drummers → voters. Attendance = enfranchisement.

Recommended v3 entry: **Snapshot-style off-chain** for speed + zero-gas voting, with DRUM Token holdings as the voting weight once DRUM originates on mainnet. "Drum to vote, vote to decide."

### 2 · Yield machine — the drip model

Mike's framing: *"pointcast is also built as a yield machine."* What's novel here vs. yet-another-staking-pool?

**Ideas worth chewing:**

**a) Attention yield** — a small drip to wallets that maintain an active presence on the site. Presence sessions → drum taps → block views → visit stamps. All of these are already tracked. Sum them, weight them, drip DRUM tokens (or tez-denominated rewards) pro-rata to wallets.

- Risk: farmable. Mitigation: cap per-wallet-per-day; require a minimum balance of Visit Nouns FA2 (so cost-of-farming = cost-of-buying a Visit Noun).
- Upside: every visitor sees their wallet tick up. It's a tiny number but it's positive and constant — the exact feeling Mike wants.

**b) Prize Cast reflow** — some % of each Prize Cast weekly draw doesn't go to the winner; it drips to Visit Nouns holders pro-rata the following week. Prize pool of 1.5 ꜩ becomes 1.2 ꜩ winner + 0.3 ꜩ dripped to ~10 holders = 0.03 ꜩ each. Small but recurring.

**c) Royalty router** — every Visit Nouns secondary sale carries a 5-10% royalty. Instead of all going to treasury, route 50% back to Visit Nouns holders (DAO members). Secondary market activity funds the DAO.

**d) DRUM yield** — DRUM holders earn a fraction of DRUM's own supply each week, proportional to held + staked balance. "The more you drum, the more DRUM you earn even when you're not drumming."

**e) Baker kickback** — every deposit into Prize Cast is staked with a baker. Some bakers kick back a % to the delegator. That kickback is small (~0.5% extra APY) but it's recurring and composes with the prize-pool yield.

**Recommendation**: combine (a) **Attention yield** as the headline mechanic (visible + small + constant drip) with (c) **Royalty router** as the funding source. Wallets that *visit* earn; the earning is funded by the secondary market activity *their collection drives*. Feels self-reinforcing.

### 3 · YeePlayer — interactive on video blocks

Mike: *"like what might be interesting to make that more interesting than just watching on YouTube https://pointcast.xyz/b/0236 ... we build a game called YeePlayer, merging a rockstyle keys style game and also the asian words over video products."*

The reference is: Guitar Hero (rhythm keys scrolling toward a hit line) × karaoke-over-video (the Korean/Japanese/Chinese caption-plus-romaji-plus-literal-translation that floats over the video frame while it plays).

**YeePlayer v0 sketch:**

- Take any WATCH-type Block with a YouTube media embed (Block 0236 chakra tune-up is the reference).
- Author (or auto-detect) "beat markers" — timestamps where a sound event happens in the video.
- Overlay a rhythm-game lane on top of the video iframe — notes scroll in sync with video time, hit line at 80% down the frame.
- Player presses keys (or taps on mobile) when notes hit the line.
- Score based on timing. Score persists to localStorage + optionally to a wallet (opens the door to DAO/yield).

**Karaoke overlay**:

- For videos with ambient/foreign-language tracks: overlay the "words" (for Temple Sounds' chakra tune-up, these could be the chakra names as they sound: LAM, VAM, RAM, YAM, HAM, OM — the seed mantras).
- Floating mono-typeset words cross the video bottom-third as the relevant section plays.
- Same beat-marker timing as the rhythm game.

**Implementation order:**

1. Static overlay prototype on /b/0236 — hard-coded timestamps, just prove the layering works over the YouTube iframe.
2. Pull out `BeatOverlay.astro` + `BeatTrack.astro` components.
3. Add `media.beats?: Array<{ t: number; note?: string; word?: string }>` to the Block schema.
4. Author one complete beat map for 0236 — should take 20 minutes with video scrubbing.
5. Add a scoring HUD. Tie to wallet via a /api/yeescore endpoint (session-based at first).

**Name sanity check**: "YeePlayer" is catchy. Could also be rendered **Yee · Player** or **yee-player.xyz** as its own sub-site later.

### 4 · Mesh — local + online

Mike: *"how do we start meshing things, mesh local, mesh online community."*

PointCast has proto-mesh primitives already:

- **Presence** — visitors are counted live. Extend this: when two wallets are on the site simultaneously, show them to each other. Chat? Signal? Emoji react?
- **NYC Mesh block (0214)** — inspiration. PointCast could be the *meta-mesh* that weaves:
  - **Local**: the El Segundo neighborhood — visit stamps with geo, future meetups (the CAPA pickleball tournament), drum sessions timed to neighborhood hours
  - **Online**: the Farcaster + X + objkt community that follows Mike's work; the mesh with other agent-native sites (llms.txt network); the mesh with Tezos NFT artists
  - **Agent**: the mesh of AI agents that read PointCast — Claude, Codex, Perplexity, GPTBot. Each leaves a visit stamp. Aggregate agent activity becomes a data product.

**Concrete v3 mesh ideas:**

- **/mesh** page — visualizes local (map of El Segundo with visit pins) + online (follow graph of cited domains in the archive) + agent (table of which LLMs have cited which Blocks, pulled from referrer logs if Cloudflare exposes them).
- **Handshake protocol** — any agent-native site with a compatible `/agents.json` can declare "I follow PointCast" and get reciprocated. Build a federation.
- **Meshlayer on Visit Blocks** — when a visit is geotagged, and another visitor is nearby, hint at the proximity: "you're 4 miles from where this visit happened." Breadcrumbs.
- **Weekly Drum Jam** — scheduled 15-minute window where the drum room counts as a single-session activity. Everyone present + drumming earns 2x DRUM yield for the week.
