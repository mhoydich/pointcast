# Manus · manus-01 · 20-person outreach list

**Priority:** can start immediately. Runs in parallel with all codex work.

## The ask

Research and compile a list of **20 specific humans** who would plausibly care about PointCast + Sparrow, with contact shape and a sentence on why each one. Not accounts with >500k followers. Not "influencers." Actual practitioners.

## Why

Mike's tweeting-at-big-accounts hasn't worked (1-view tags). Cold DMs to specific people who work in the intersections PointCast touches is the unglamorous thing that actually moves the needle. Twenty direct touches ≫ twenty thousand tagged impressions.

## Target communities + counts

- **Nostr protocol / client builders (6):** people shipping Nostr clients or writing about the protocol. Think: fiatjaf, Jack Chakany (nos2x / damus), Alex Gleason (Ditto), Pablo (snort/shipyard), Giszmo, etc. Prefer active posters with demonstrated technical depth, not top-of-feed noise accounts.
- **Astro core / ecosystem (4):** Astro maintainers + prominent Astro-adjacent bloggers (Fred K Schott, Ben Holmes, Jason Lengstorf, Matt Kane). All publicly reachable on Bluesky / X.
- **Agentic coding / AI-builds-in-public (5):** people documenting AI-pair-programming workflows, Anthropic devrel, Simon Willison, Swyx, Geoffrey Huntley. Anyone who'd appreciate the "Claude ships a sprint every 15 min" angle.
- **Indie-web / small-web writers (3):** Robin Sloan, Tom MacWright, Simon Sarris — people who understand the "living broadcast" framing and write for similar audiences.
- **One or two long-shots (2):** technologist-novelists or cultural critics (Robin Rendle, Nadia Asparouhova) who'd find the PointCast editorial layer genuinely interesting.

## Per-entry shape

```md
## {N}. {Name} · {Handle}
- **Role:** {One line.}
- **Why they'd care:** {Which angle lands with them — Nostr, Astro, agentic, cultural.}
- **Contact:**
  - primary: {Bluesky @ | X @ | email | Nostr npub}
  - backup: {…}
- **Opening line (draft):** 2-3 sentences they'd respond to. Not a sales pitch; a relevant observation + a single link. For example: "I saw your piece on Astro's zero-JS posture. I built a reader with 0 kB of framework runtime that federates reading lists over Nostr — pointcast.xyz/sparrow/friends. Curious what you'd want different in the manifest shape."
- **Risk:** {Anything to watch for — account that hates DMs, known to ignore cold reach, recent public stance that matters.}
```

## Rules of engagement

- **No generic language.** Each opener must reference something the recipient actually did or said.
- **Single link, not a tour.** One URL per DM. Lead with `/sparrow/friends` for federation people, `/sparrow/signals` for the Nostr RFC people, `/for-agents` for the agentic-coding people.
- **No tags, no @-bait.** Direct message or a genuine reply to a real post of theirs.
- **Twitter is last resort.** Bluesky and Nostr DMs have higher open rates right now for this audience.
