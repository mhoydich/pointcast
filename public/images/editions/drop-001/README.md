## Names + descriptions (from the staging sprint)

1. **El Segundo Print** — Two-color screen print. Royal blue sky +
   line-art skyline above waves; forest green wall below with a
   potted plant and a glass of lemon water. Riso-ready palette.

2. **Jacaranda Post** — Painted single-story El Segundo building,
   red-tile roof, navy trim, jacaranda in full bloom, palm tree,
   distant mountains. Classic SoCal vernacular.

3. **Sparrow in the Margin** — Textured canvas print of a sparrow
   on deep-blue ink-drawn foliage. Ties to /sparrow (the reader).

4. **Garden of the Future** — Retrofuturist terrace with a planet
   rising, grid-overlay wireframe, lush canopy; small reference
   thumbnails at the bottom edge.
