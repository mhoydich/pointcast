玩法

HOW TO PLAY

落單 · 焗爐 1 · ORDERS & OVENS

揀右手邊嘅落單(撳或者 QWERT),然後撳一個空爐(1234)開工。 Pick an order on the right, then click an empty oven to start baking. Hotkeys QWERT for orders, 1–4 for ovens.

出爐時機 2 · BAKE TIMING

等焗條入翠綠位(PERFECT)先撳再出爐。太早 = 生,太遲 = 燶。正中紅心 = BULLSEYE,花紅多啲。 Click when the fill enters the jade band. Too early = RAW, too late = BURNT. Dead-center = BULLSEYE with bonus cash.

RAW 生PERFECT 剛剛好BURNT 燶

VIP 金單 3 · VIP ORDERS

★ VIP ★ 金色落單罕見,工錢 3×。但必須焗到 PERFECT,否則風頭 +18。 Rare gold orders pay 3×. Must be PERFECT — anything less = +18 heat and a furious buyer.

查牌 · 收起 4 · RAIDS & HIDING

RAID 差人一到,3.5 秒內撳 SPACE 或者 收起!。遲咗就風頭 +26。買咗賄賂就自動過關。 When the inspector hits, slam SPACE or HIDE! within 3.5s. Miss it = +26 heat. A bribe stack auto-skips the next raid.

夜半市 5 · NIGHT SHOP

每晚完咗去夜半市舖頭洗錢買裝備:加寬爐位、快爐、第四爐、阿姐茶、賄賂、上鎖房、睇水細佬、黑市門路。 Between nights, the Night Market sells upgrades: wider sweet spot, fast ovens, 4th oven, combo tea, bribes, padlock, lookout, VIP connection.

特別夜 · 風頭 6 · EVENTS & HEAT

每晚有特別事件 — 打風(+50% 錢)、大時大節(多單)、平靜、查牌之夜(多查牌)、黑幫收陀($300 或 +30 風頭)。風頭 100 = BUSTED。 Each night rolls an event — typhoon, festival, quiet, inspection, shakedown. Heat at 100 = BUSTED.
