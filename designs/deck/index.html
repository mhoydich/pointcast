Dispatch●Why bother
Social media is flattening.
Platforms eat what you publish.
Art becomes “content.”
i.

The feed is a blender.

Every post is reformatted into the same 1:1 square, the same algorithmic rhythm.

ii.

Platforms keep the keys.

Your audience, your reach, your archive — rented from someone else's server.

iii.

Art is renamed “content.”

A song, a painting, a joke — all flattened into the same unit of attention.
